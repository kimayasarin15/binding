The Power and Politics of Design

  1. Disruption
  2. Navigate
  3. Reveal

The three articles discuss the ethics and power of design. Across all texts, there is shared understanding that design is never neutral: it shapes systems and ideologies. Disrupt calls for designers to be unpredictable and courageous. Navigate underscores the tensions of working within commercial systems. Reveal exposes how design constructs our social norms. Together, they are in conversation about the role of designers, and design more broadly, in everyday life.
